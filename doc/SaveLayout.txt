Save Layout:

0x00000     OoT Save data
0x08000     MM Save 1 + backup
0x0c000     MM Save 2 + backup
0x10000     MM Owl 1
0x14000     MM Owl 1 backup
0x18000     MM Owl 2
0x1c000     MM Owl 2 backup
0x20000     MM Settings
0x24000     MM Reserved
0x30000     Combo Save 1
0x34000     Combo Save 2
0x38000     Combo Save 3
